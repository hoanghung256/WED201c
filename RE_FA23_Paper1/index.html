Unforgettable about Ha Giang

Being a high mountains province, the climate in Hà Giang is noticeably cold compares to the neighboring lowlands and midlands. the average annual temperature is about 21-23 degrees Celsius. Outstanding features of the climate are high humidity throught out the year, heavy and prolonged rain.

You can travel to Ha Giang in any season of the year. People often come to Ha Giang in the fall when the rice is ripe and golden. The most beautiful time is October, November and December, when buckwheat flowers or mustard feilds bloom. In spring, apricot blossoms and plum blossoms bloom white in the forest, making you feel like you're floating on the clouds.

Outstanding features of the climate are high humidity throught out the year, heavy and prolonged rain. Năm 1900, Đạo quan binh Hà Giang đổi thành tỉnh Hà Giang.
